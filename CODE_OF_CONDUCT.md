# Code of Conduct

## Pledge

I want this project to be a welcoming, respectful place for everyone.

Participants are expected to act professionally and constructively in issues, pull requests, and other project spaces.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and patient
- Give actionable, technical feedback
- Assume good intent unless there is clear evidence otherwise
- Focus on the work, not personal attacks

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated bad-faith engagement

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security-style report using the process in `SECURITY.md` or contact the maintainers directly through the repository contact channels.
